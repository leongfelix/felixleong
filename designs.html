Hydrogen Fuel Cell Test Stations
  • As hydrogen fuel cell technology advances, testing stations must equally advance so that new milestones can be reached. I designed hydrogen fuel cell test stations for multiple Tier 1 manufacturers and universities, custom-tailored to meet each client's specific fuel cell testing needs. Each station is designed with utmost attention to equipment (control valves, vessels, etc) and piping.
    • Control Valves
  • I reverse-engineered and improved an existing design of a control valve. Upon receiving notice that the failure rate of this model was 2.37% in 2017, I applied Six Sigma problem solving principles to locate the problem and successfully applied design changes to reduce the failure rate to 0.37% in 2018.
    • Level Controller
  • I designed a new level controller to cater to a new market. As this product is classified as a pressure containing product, I submitted a report application to the regulatory body ABSA demonstrating design calculations and standards compliance to receive regulatory approval. The product was successfully registered and a CRN was obtained
    • Essential Mechanical Components
  • As a Mechanical Engineer, I understand the principles and design of major essential mechanical components. I designed and manufactured these components according to their principle codes and standards, such as API and ASME. These include fittings, tubings, casing, valves, rods, etc.
    • Composite Underground Storage Tanks
  • I designed a new composite storage tank according to ASME Section X standards. This includes destructively testing the tank to validate the design.
