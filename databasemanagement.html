HIGHLIGHTS - FELIX LEONG - Database Management

SAP databases are commonly used as a central storage location of asset information for large companies. It provides a platform for equipment, maintenance, purchasing, and all other adaptable functions. Businesses looking to implement an SAP system from ground-up while deep into operations face a complicated task of information gathering, consolidation, defining, sorting, hierarchies, and uploading. This is especially true when multiple business functions are involved with different types of assets and a limited timeframe.

I was able to provide the groundwork for my client's SAP system in a year-long project, by utilizing my knowledge of Excel-based databases, codings and algorithms, and logical mindmapping. This was comprised of assets from over 13 sites around Canada, with more than 10,000 unique assets in total.
