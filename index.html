Versatility, flexibility, and creativity - these competencies are what I bring with my design and manufacturing skillset. I have worked at a comprehensive variety of production facilities, whether it be low or high volume production, simplistic parts or complex systems, changing manufacturing or workflow processes, or managing product lines or development projects.

I develop products with a high level of competency utilizing my 3D modelling expertise, knowledge of industry regulation and standards, attention to manufacturability, and simulations such as finite element. In facilities, I have been the key contact for engineering and manufacturing support from various technical and non-technical teams, such as sales, quality, production, and maintenance to ensure plant processes run without interruption. As a project lead, I have led multiple successful projects for clients by tracking budget, deliverables, and timelines.

Outside of my professional life, I am fascinated by the art of movements, whether it be the emotional capabilities of dance or the rigidity of powerlifting form.
