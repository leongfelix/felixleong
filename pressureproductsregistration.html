HIGHLIGHTS - FELIX LEONG - Pressure Products Registration

Pressure retaining components are vital to the operation of fluid systems, and must be registered with a provincial safety manager as per the Safety Standards Act in Canada. Navigating through the required documentation and codes of compliance can be a daunting task, requiring a multitude of cross-references between codes.

I have reviewed the requirements of both Technical Safety BC and Alberta Boilers Safety Assocation, and have successfully registered vessels and fittings by submitting the required documentation and proof of compliance. In the following section, I have provided a table of my personal notes on the matter. If you are looking for a comprehensive review and registration of your product, please get in touch with me.

Criteria Code (Pressure Vessels Only) Reference

Head Wall Thickness ASME Section VIII-1 Appendix 1-4
